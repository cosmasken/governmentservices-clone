No personally identifiable information is automatically collected about visitors who simply browse this site or who download information from it. eCitizen does not release any information about the collection of IP addresses to any third party, except under court order or as required by law. We may share the aggregate (statistical) information with third parties without reference to individual information. We may e-mail you regarding billing or service-related issues.

Digital Payments

The Government of Kenya shall endeavour to provide digital payments modes to citizens via all viable payment channels by availing payments via all banks, mobile payment platforms as well as payments from other financial services providers where citizens hold funds. The use of the eCitizen services is subject to a convenience fee that shall go towards the provision of these services and may vary from service to service on a per transaction basis. You agree that a payment instruction that is given before the cut-off time for a banking business day will be processed on the same day but if it is given after the cut-off time it may be processed on the next banking business day after the instruction is given. Should your payment service provider reverse your payment for any reason, the lodgement made in relation to that transaction may also be reversed. This could have implications for the granting/continuation of your licence. You are responsible for ensuring that payments are made only using valid credit cards.

Refund Policy

In the event that your payment to the website was charged to your credit card in error, we will honor your request to refund the original transaction and refund your payment in full, as long as the request is made within ninety days from the transaction processing date. The period of ninety days will begin from the transaction processing date. Be aware that credit card refunds can take anywhere from 3-10 days to reflect on your credit card account. We reserve the right to revise this policy at any time.
